**Kollywood** is the informal name for the **Tamil-language film industry**, based primarily in Kodambakkam, a neighborhood in Chennai that historically housed many film studios. The name combines **Kodambakkam** and **Hollywood**.

Here's an overview:

### History

* Tamil cinema began in the early 20th century with silent films.
* The first Tamil talkie, Kalidas, was released in 1931.
* During the 1950s–1970s, Tamil cinema grew rapidly with influential actors, directors, and screenwriters who also shaped Tamil Nadu's politics.

### Music

Tamil cinema has produced internationally recognized composers, including:

* Ilaiyaraaja
* A. R. Rahman, who won multiple Academy Awards.
* Anirudh Ravichander
